We typically respond within two to three business days. For urgent matters, please mark your subject line with [URGENT].

What to Include in Your Message

To help us respond quickly and usefully, a little context goes a long way:

🐛 Bug Reports

Let us know your browser and operating system, what you were trying to do, and what happened instead. Screenshots help.

💡 Feature Requests

Tell us what you are trying to accomplish, not just the feature you imagine. Sometimes there is a simpler solution.

🌍 New City Requests

Include the city name, country, and time zone. We try to add popular requests in our periodic updates.

🔒 Privacy Questions

Anything about data handling, cookies, advertising, or our privacy policy. We take these seriously.

Before You Write

Many common questions are answered on our homepage's FAQ section. You might find your answer there instantly — check the FAQ first, especially for questions about alarm sounds, timezone accuracy, or whether timers survive a page reload.

Business Inquiries

For advertising partnerships outside of AdSense, sponsorships, or licensing inquiries, please use the same email address with [BUSINESS] in the subject line.

Press and Media

We are happy to answer press questions, provide screenshots, or speak about our approach to building simple, private web tools. Use [PRESS] in your subject line.

A Note on Response Time

WorldClock is run by a small team and email is answered during normal business hours in the local time zone. Weekends and major holidays will see delays. Please be patient — we do read everything, and thoughtful messages get thoughtful replies.
